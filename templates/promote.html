Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your impression estimates?
Our estimates are based on historical data from similar promotions during comparable time periods. While we strive for accuracy, many factors can affect actual performance including seasonality, market trends, and listing quality. We provide a realistic range rather than a guaranteed number.
What should I do if my campaign is underperforming?
First, check your campaign analytics to understand performance. If impressions are significantly below estimates, contact our support team within 3 days of campaign completion. We'll review your campaign and apply our guarantee policy if applicable.
Can I get a refund if impressions are low?
While we don't offer direct refunds, our guarantee provides significant value if impressions fall short.
