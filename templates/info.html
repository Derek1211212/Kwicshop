About Sellkwic

SellKwic is the simplest way to launch an e‑commerce store – without writing a single line of code. Founded in 2024, our mission is to empower entrepreneurs across Africa and beyond to sell online with ease.

We provide beautiful storefronts, smart analytics, and marketing tools – all in one platform. Whether you're selling fashion, electronics, or handmade goods, SellKwic gives you the freedom to focus on your products while we handle the technology.

🌍 Our Vision

To become the go‑to platform for zero‑code commerce in emerging markets, enabling anyone to start and grow their online business.

✨ Why SellKwic?

  • Setup in under 3 minutes – no technical skills required.
  • Free to start, with affordable upgrades as you grow.
  • Local payment methods and support for multiple currencies (coming soon).
  • Dedicated customer support and a community of like‑sized sellers.

We're headquartered in Accra, Ghana, with remote teams across the continent. Join over 15,000 merchants who already trust SellKwic.
